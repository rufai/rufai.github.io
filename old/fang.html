52-Week FAANG Preparation Outcomes

Student Capabilities

Upon completion, students will be FAANG-ready:

  • Algorithmic Mastery: Solved 200+ LeetCode problems across all difficulty levels with a deep understanding of time/space complexity.
  • Data Structure Proficiency: Can implement and explain core data structures (Arrays, Trees, Graphs, etc.) from scratch.
  • System Design Excellence: Can design and whiteboard large-scale distributed systems (URL shorteners, news feeds, chat, ride-hailing) and articulate trade-offs.
  • Specialized Knowledge: Developed deep expertise in a chosen domain (e.g., Databases, Networking) demonstrated through a complex "Bar Raiser" project.
  • Behavioral Interview Readiness: Can structure compelling answers using the STAR method for leadership, conflict resolution, and project impact questions.
  • Production-Quality Project: Built a unique, well-documented, and deployed "Bar Raiser" project that serves as a powerful talking point and portfolio centerpiece.
  • Interview Confidence: Completed numerous mock interviews and is prepared to handle the pressure and pace of a real FAANG interview loop.

Important Note: This intensive plan is designed to maximize FAANG interview success while building a strong EB1-A portfolio. Success requires consistent effort.
